SpaceX is a company. This company makes rockets. Elon Musk is the boss of this company.
The company launches a rocket. It goes to space. It carries a satellite. This part goes fine. The rocket then goes back to Earth.
Elon Musk puts a video on the Internet. It shows the rocket. It is landing. The rocket has four legs. One leg is broken. The rocket falls down and explodes.
Difficult words: company (business), launch (to send into the air), Earth (our planet), land (to come down from the air onto the ground).
